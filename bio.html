My Biography

Who am I?

I am currently studying at the University of Northampton in the first year of Computer Games Development. In my spare time I like to write programs in a variety of programming languages including Java, Action Script, but most of all C#. The main and favourite focus of my programming is computer games, from Tower Defence to Factory games.

I also like to try and keep fit by taking part in once a week Badminton sessions, as well as cycling (both out doors and on a machine) and I also try and get outside for hour long walks on days when I have time to. Other activities which I do outside of University include volunteering at the British Heart Foundation. During the holidays I help out for at least two mornings a week.

I aspire to become a professional computer games programmer who either works in a small company or for a much larger corporation.
